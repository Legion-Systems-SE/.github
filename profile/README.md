## Method

Every number has a digit sequence. The second finite difference of that
sequence — its curvature — encodes structural information independent of
magnitude. Two numbers resonate when the dot product of their curvatures
satisfies an arithmetic progression condition.

The engine and the calculator are two sides of the same framework: the
engine evolves fields seeded by zeta zeros and measures what emerges; the
calculator tests whether the emergent structure is visible in the digit
sequences of physical constants.
